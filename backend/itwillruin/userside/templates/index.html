From Challenge to Solution

Born from the NASA Space Apps Challenge to make Earth observation data useful for everyone.

The Problem

Standard forecasts are short-term and don't quantify the historical risk of "very hot," "very cold," or "very wet" conditions for a specific day months in advance, making long-term planning a gamble.

Our Solution

Our app provides a personalized interface to query NASA's historical data. Pinpoint a location and date, and we provide the statistical likelihood of various weather conditions, starting with precipitation.

Key Features

Customized Forecast

Define your own thresholds for "very hot" or "very windy" to get a forecast tailored to your specific needs and comfort levels.

Global Coverage

Explore weather patterns and get long-term forecasts for any location on the globe, thanks to NASA's extensive satellite network.

Clear Probabilities

We don't just say "it might rain." We provide clear, data-driven probabilities for different conditions to help you make truly informed decisions.

A NASA Space Apps Challenge Solution

This project was developed for the NASA Space Apps Challenge to bridge the gap between complex satellite data and public use. Our application is built directly on NASA's rich repository of Earth observation data, utilizing the POWER API and IMERG datasets to provide reliable, long-term weather probabilities for any location on Earth.
