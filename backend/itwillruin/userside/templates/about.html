Our Mission

Bridging Space Data and Your Daily Plans

We believe the vast knowledge gathered by NASA's Earth observation satellites shouldn't be confined to scientific papers. Our mission is to translate this complex, vital data into simple, actionable insights for your everyday life, helping you plan your most important events with confidence.

The Technology Behind the Forecast

We use a powerful combination of trusted data sources and cutting-edge AI.

NASA POWER API

The foundation of our predictions. We tap into decades of global meteorological data from NASA's Prediction of Worldwide Energy Resources project for scientifically-backed weather insights.

Gemini AI

Data is only useful when you can understand it. We use Google's Gemini AI to analyze the weather data and generate human-friendly advice, contingency plans, and even fun facts.

Modern Web Stack

Built with a robust Django backend and an interactive frontend using Leaflet for mapping, ensuring a scalable, reliable, and user-friendly experience across all devices.
