About RainCast

Transparency for the NASA Space Apps Challenge.

Why Precipitation Matters

Precipitation is a key factor in our daily lives, impacting everything from agriculture and water resource management to event planning and disaster preparedness. By providing accurate and accessible precipitation forecasts, we aim to help people make more informed decisions in the face of a changing climate.

NASA Data

The project was developed using Python with Pandas, Statsmodels, and Requests for data handling and modeling, and Django as the web framework.

All datasets were sourced directly from NASA POWER API , which provided daily weather variables (temperature, precipitation, wind speed, and humidity) spanning from the year 2000 to the present.

These datasets were aggregated monthly to feed the time series models.
