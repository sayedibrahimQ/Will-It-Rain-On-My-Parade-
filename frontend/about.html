About It Will Ruin

This project was created for the NASA Space Apps Challenge. Our goal is to provide users with a simple and intuitive tool to predict the probability of extreme weather events for a given location and date.

Data Sources

We use a variety of data sources from NASA to power our predictions. These include:

  • NASA GPM (Global Precipitation Measurement): Provides global observations of rain and snow.
  • NASA MERRA-2 (Modern-Era Retrospective analysis for Research and Applications, Version 2): A global atmospheric reanalysis that provides a wide range of weather and climate data.
  • NASA POWER (Prediction of Worldwide Energy Resources): Provides solar and meteorological data sets for renewable energy and agricultural applications.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make complex climate data accessible and understandable to everyone. By providing probabilistic forecasts of extreme weather, we hope to help people make more informed decisions and prepare for the potential impacts of climate change.
