About RainCast

Transparency for the NASA Space Apps Challenge.

Why Precipitation Matters

Precipitation is a key factor in our daily lives, impacting everything from agriculture and water resource management to event planning and disaster preparedness. By providing accurate and accessible precipitation forecasts, we aim to help people make more informed decisions in the face of a changing climate.

NASA Data

This project is powered by the Integrated Multi-satellitE Retrievals for GPM (IMERG) V07 dataset. We access the data through OPeNDAP.

IMERG combines information from the GPM satellite constellation to estimate precipitation over the majority of the Earth's surface. The data provides precipitation estimates every 30 minutes.

Technology Stack

  • Backend: Django (Python)
  • Styling: Custom CSS with a modern, minimal design
  • Mapping: Leaflet.js
