Understanding the Data

Our predictions are based on NASA's Integrated Multi-satellitE Retrievals for GPM (IMERG) dataset. The values for precipitation are given in mm/hr. The probability of liquid precipitation and the random error associated with the measurement are also provided.

It's important to note that these are probabilistic forecasts, and while they are based on advanced satellite data, they come with a degree of uncertainty. The 'randomError' value gives an indication of this uncertainty.
