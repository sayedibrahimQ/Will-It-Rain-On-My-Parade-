The NASA Space Apps Challenge

Our project addresses the challenge of making Earth observation data useful for personal planning, like vacations, hikes, or outdoor events.

The Problem

When planning an outdoor activity, it's hard to know the chances of adverse weather. Standard forecasts don't always tell you the likelihood of "very hot", "very cold", or "very wet" conditions for your specific needs.

Our Solution

EventWeather provides a personalized interface to query NASA's Earth observation data. You specify the location and date, and our app provides the likelihood of various weather conditions, starting with precipitation.

Key Features

Customized Forecast

Define your own thresholds for "very hot" or "very windy" and get a forecast tailored to your comfort.

Global Coverage

Explore weather patterns and get forecasts for any location on the globe, thanks to NASA's satellite network.

Clear Probabilities

We don't just say "it might rain." We provide clear probabilities for different conditions to help you make informed decisions.

Powered by NASA Data

Our application is built upon NASA's rich repository of Earth observation data, starting with the IMERG dataset for precipitation. Our platform is designed to integrate further datasets for temperature, wind, and more.
